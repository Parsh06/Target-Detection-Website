Why Robust Human Target Detection ?

In today's world, where security and surveillance have become increasingly important, it is crucial to have accurate and reliable methods for detecting and tracking human targets.

Age Detection

Our cutting-edge technology includes age detection capabilities. This feature allows us to estimate the age of individuals accurately. Age detection can be valuable in various scenarios, including marketing research, access control, and public safety.

Gender Detection

In addition to age detection, our technology offers gender detection capabilities. It can accurately determine the gender of individuals. Gender detection has applications in fields such as marketing, retail, and security, where understanding the gender demographics of a crowd is essential for decision-making.

Night Vision

Our technology incorporates advanced night vision capabilities, allowing for effective human target detection even in low-light and nighttime conditions. Night vision technology enhances security and surveillance operations, making them more reliable around the clock.

Threat Level Alert

We prioritize threat detection and alerting to enhance security measures. Our system utilizes a color-coded threat level alert system:

  • Yellow: Indicates human detection, providing initial awareness of human presence.
  • Orange: Indicates the detection of one anomaly, signaling a potential irregularity or security concern.
  • Red: Activated when two or more anomalies are detected, signifying a higher level of security threat.

This threat level alert system ensures timely responses and better decision-making, allowing for effective security management in various critical scenarios.
